Senior Under Officer

Senior Under Officer:Highest cadet rank, responsible for leadership and guidance.

Company Junior Under Officer

Company Junior Under Officer:second highest cadet rank, responsible for leadership and guidance.

Company Sergent Master

Responsible for the discipline and training of a company of cadets

Company Quartermaster Sergeant (CQMS)

Manages the logistics and supplies within a company..

Sargent

Non-commissioned officer overseeing a section of cadets.

Corporal (Cpl)

Assists the Sergeant in training and management.

Lance Corporal(LCPL)

Assists the Sergeant and CPL in training and management..

Cadet

The entry or initial rank for cadets in the National Cadet Corps (NCC) is usually simply referred to as "Cadet." It represents the starting point for individuals who join the NCC program.
